Your family's data,

DataFlow is a self-hosted, encrypted data vault for passwords, documents, and sensitive family information. No cloud, no subscriptions — your data stays on your machine.

Get Started Learn More

Built for families. Built for security.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

AES-256-GCM encryption with Argon2 key derivation. Your data is encrypted at rest and only decrypted in memory.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Roles

Admin, adult, and child roles. Share specific items or categories with family members securely.

📋

Flexible Record Types

Passwords, credit cards, notes, identities, medical records — built-in and custom record types.

🔑

Password Generator

Generate strong passwords or memorable passphrases. Configurable length, character sets, and separators.

📊

Audit Trail

Every action is logged. Know who accessed what, when, and from where — full accountability.

🏠

Self-Hosted

Run on your own hardware. SQLite-backed, single binary, no external dependencies. Fully offline-capable.
